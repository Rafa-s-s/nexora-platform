# Nexora Product Roadmap

This document presents the planned evolution roadmap of the Nexora Platform.

The roadmap was created to guide the transformation of Nexora from an MVP WhatsApp chatbot into a scalable SaaS platform focused on AI-assisted communication and organizational support automation.

---

# Product Vision

Nexora aims to become a centralized WhatsApp-first communication platform capable of combining:

- chatbot automation
- AI-assisted support
- human support handoff
- operational dashboards
- scalable SaaS infrastructure
- accessible communication

The platform is initially focused on NGOs and social organizations, but its architecture allows expansion into other business sectors.

---

# Phase 1 — MVP Foundation

## Objective

Validate the core concept of WhatsApp automation for organizations.

## Main Goals

- WhatsApp integration
- chatbot menu
- automated FAQ
- donation flow
- volunteer flow
- project information flow
- human support handoff
- basic dashboard
- conversation storage

## Technologies

- WAHA
- n8n
- Docker
- PostgreSQL

## Status

In development.

# Phase 2 — Dashboard Expansion

- administrator management

- audit logs

- screen reader compatibility

- improved UX for elderly users

- advanced authentication
- role-based access control
- encrypted sessions
